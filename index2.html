IT Course

IT introduction:Information Technology covers a broad spectrum of hardware and software solutions that enable organizations to gather, organize, and analyze data that helps them achieve their goals. It also details technology-based workflow processes that expand the capacity of an organization to deliver services that generate revenue. Professional web design needs to go through the following stages: It is necessary to conduct a comprehensive analysis according to the needs of consumers, the situation of the market, and the situation of the enterprise itself, so as to establish a marketing model. Carry out functional planning with business goals as the center, and make a column structure diagram. Here are the steps to create a website: The functional specifications that need to be evaluated for use; Competitive research; develop the structure (tree), content and presentation of web pages; Create a web interface (also known as web design); Website integration in different programming languages. The main HTML (an application under the standard general markup language), Cascading Style Sheets (foreign language abbreviation: CSS), JavaScript, PHP, ASP.NET; .web test Performance Testing (1) Connection speed test. The speed at which users connect to the e-commerce network is related to the way they surf the Internet, they may be dial-up telephones, or broadband Internet access. (2) Load test. Load testing is to detect the actual performance of the e-commerce system under a certain load level. That is, how many users can be allowed to be online at the same time, and the load can be tested by simulating multiple users on a client through the corresponding software. (3) Stress test. Stress testing is to test the system's limitations and failure recovery capabilities, that is, to test whether the e-commerce system will crash. security testing.

Finance

Finance is a broad term describing activities related to banking, leverage or debt, credit, capital markets, currency and investing. Basically, treasury represents the process of money management and getting the money you need. Finance also includes the supervision, creation and study of money, banking, credit, investments, assets and liabilities that make up the financial system. Many fundamental concepts in finance have their roots in microeconomic and macroeconomic theory. One of the most basic theories is the time value of money, which basically states that a dollar today is worth more than a dollar in the future.
